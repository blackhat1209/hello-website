Lesson 1: Wave–Particle Duality

Wave–particle duality is the concept that light and matter can behave both as waves and as particles.

Example

Light sometimes behaves like a wave (interference patterns) and sometimes like particles called photons.

Key Formula

De Broglie wavelength:

λ = h / p

Quick Question

What does wave–particle duality mean?

Wave–particle duality means particles like electrons and photons can behave both as waves and as particles.
